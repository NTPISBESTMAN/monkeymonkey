Making the lasagna

Making the lasagna might be very hard at first, but through pratice it will become easier.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. COok the noodles
  3. make the ricottta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna according to the recipes instructions
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
